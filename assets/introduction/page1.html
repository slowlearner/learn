Introduction


Abu Jafar Muhammad Ibn Musu Al-Khowarizmi define the Design and Analysis of Algorithm:

1. A set of rules for carrying out calculation either by hand or on a machine.

2. A finite step-by-step procedure to achieve a required result.

3. A sequence of computational steps that transform the input into the output.

4. A sequence of operations performed on data that have to be organized in data structures and

5. An abstraction of a program to be executed on a physical machine also called a model of computation.
